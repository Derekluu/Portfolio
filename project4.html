About Me
-------------------------------------------------------
Hi I'm Derek Luu and I am an artist studying at San Jose State University. I enjoy creating digital artworks and am able to use a variety of tools like Clip Studio Paint, Photoshop, Illustrator, Animate, Premier, Blender, Maya, and much more. I am still learning and hope to be able to expand my skillset more to help create more visually appealing artworks based through my love of story telling.
