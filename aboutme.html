Artist Biography

Derek Luu is a Vietnamese American who was born and raised in the Bay Area and has been making art since 2019. He is a digital artist who focuses on web design, illustrations, and animations. He draws inspiration from games, animated films, comics, and more. He strives to create works of art that are funny, cute, or cool to entertain friends, family, and strangers. Growing up games, animations, and other related works that are found on the internet have always been a form of escape for Derek. Being able to de-stress and take things step by step he strives to be able to create cool films, funny websites, or cute comics to offer people comfort and entertainment. .
