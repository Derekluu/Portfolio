Statement

I enjoy drawing and creating illustrations for people to make people happy, laugh, and feel a sense of satisfaction. My artworks are created through the use of digital media, primarily Clip Studio Paint EX. I create interesting digital illustrations that range from fun and simple to complex and fantastical. I also bring and create new ideas through the use of character designs, conceptual designs, and world-building. I enjoy drawing out and exploring ideas from the real world and reflecting them in a made-up fantasy world. To do that, I tone down topics and represent them in a fun and interesting way for entertainment. I want viewers to be entertained in a light-hearted way. The big reason why I draw for entertainment is to lighten the mood, because the real world has many serious topics.
