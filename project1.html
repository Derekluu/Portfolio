Artist Statement

Glitch Art

Tools Used:

Photoshop

Notepad

Project Description: In this project we learned the very basics of manipulating the meta data of image files. In the first piece I took an image of paint on canvas and opened the file into a text editor. In that text editor is a bunch of data that I then deleted, replaced, moved, and added to create glitches to the image file. I then took this glitched image and put it into photoshop to further edit the photo to my liking. I was able to rehash the composition and colors to my liking. The proside of editing an image in a notepad I believe is its chaotic randomness but with little control. Putting it into photoshop gave me extra control to play with the randomness created. The last two images are the result of this combined work.
